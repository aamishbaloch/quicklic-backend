Quicklic provides a simple, yet efficient platform for doctors and patients to connect with each other. Quicklic is the ultimate replacement for any and all manual logistics for a hospital automating their entire scheduling processes.

How It Works

It allows patients to find the right doctor, schedule and keep track of their appointments, and also view doctor’s notes later on. This app works as a smart personal assistant for doctors, letting them know about the day’s schedule, upcoming appointments, and patient details.

1

Book Appointments

Quicklic allows you to book appointments with your doctor at the time of your choice very easily.

2

Get Notifications

You will get notifications when the doctor accepts the appointment, and when the appointment is near.

3

View Visit history

You can view the entire history of your visits along with notes added by the doctors against it..

Features

Quicklic hosts some of the most amazing features to make your appointment scheduling experience as smooth as possible.

Book Appointment with Any Doctor

Now you can select and book appointment for any doctor for a clinic at any time slot.

Appointment History

View a history of all your appointments, and filter as needed.

Push Notifications

Receive notifications on every step of the appointment to always be up to date about the progress.

Medical Tests

View information about medical tests along with prices and best locations.

Smart stats for doctors

Doctors receive smart stats and actionable insights for all their patients.

24/7 Support

We provide 24/7 support to doctors and clinics for all their queries and concerns.
